Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and boil lasagna noodles until al dente in salted water.
  2. Brown ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet, then drain excess fat.
  3. Layer tomato sauce, noodles, meat, ricotta, and cheeses in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Repeat layering process, then cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Remove foil and bake for additional 10-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden brown; let cool before serving.
