Adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or pork belly
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Whole black peppercorns
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken or pork with soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves for 30 minutes.
  2. Sear marinated meat until golden brown in a hot skillet or pot.
  3. Add water, simmer uncovered for 30-40 minutes until tender.
  4. Adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or vinegar as needed.
  5. Serve hot with rice, garnish with green onions if desired.
