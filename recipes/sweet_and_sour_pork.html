Sweet and Sour Pork

Ingredients

  • Pork loin or pork tenderloin, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Bell peppers
  • Sweet and sour sauce
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cut pork into bite-sized pieces, coat with cornstarch, and fry until golden brown.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© bell peppers until slightly tender.
  3. Add pineapple chunks and sweet and sour sauce to the pan, simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Once the sauce thickens slightly, add fried pork to the pan, toss to coat.
  5. Serve hot over rice and enjoy your delicious sweet and sour pork!
